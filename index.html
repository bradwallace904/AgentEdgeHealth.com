About Us

AgentEdge Health is an AI‑enablement service that makes specialty medical practices discoverable and bookable via AI assistants—voice agents, chatbots, and digital assistants. As AI-driven search and scheduling become the norm in healthcare, private practice websites often remain invisible because they lack structured content and automated booking pathways. AgentEdge upgrades physicians’ online presence for the age of AI, ensuring assistants like Siri, Alexa, and ChatGPT can find your practice, understand your services, and schedule patients directly.
